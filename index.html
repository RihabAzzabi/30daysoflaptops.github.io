Why do kids need laptops?

Increasing early access to computers and coding can change lives forever.

Here at Codestarter, we believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to learn. We crowdfund $250 laptops for kids that want to learn to code, but can't afford their own computer. Through partnerships with schools in underserved communities and organizations like CoderDojo, we distribute the laptops to kids and help get them started in coding classes. Want to learn more? Check out our website: https://codestarter.org

The only way our #30DaysofLaptops will work, is if you pass this challenge on to another company within 24 hours and keep our game of tag going. Let’s use these next 30 days to get laptops to as many hopeful and excited kids as possible!
